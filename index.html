Our Story
Redefining Urban Parking in Dombivli, One Slot at a Time

Autospot Parking System was founded on a single powerful belief — that parking your vehicle should be the easiest part of your day, not the most stressful. We saw urban drivers in Dombivli waste precious time searching for spaces, fumbling with cash, battling queues, and spending their entire visit worrying about their vehicle's safety. We decided to change all of that — from the ground up.

Today, our facility operates as a fully cashless, technology-first parking ecosystem. Powered by NHAI-compliant FASTag RFID readers at every entry and exit gate, our system automatically identifies your vehicle the moment it approaches — fetching your name, booking reference, assigned slot number, and completing the payment transaction in under two seconds. No tokens. No queues. No cash. Not now, not ever.

Our commitment doesn't end at the gate. Inside our premises, every single parking slot is equipped with its own individual buzzer alarm sensor. Any unauthorized access, unexpected vehicular movement, or tampering with a parked vehicle instantly triggers a loud audible alert and simultaneously notifies our 24/7 security control room — guaranteeing a sub-60-second physical response. Multiple overlapping CCTV zones ensure complete visual accountability at all times.

When we say "Your Vehicle, Our Responsibility" — we mean it as a corporate guarantee, not a marketing line. From the moment your car enters our gates to the moment it drives back out, its protection is entirely, unconditionally, in our hands.

🔔
Buzzer Alarm System
Each parking slot is individually fitted with a high-sensitivity motion and contact sensor. Any unauthorized entry or suspicious activity triggers an immediate loud alarm and an instant alert to our security control room — guaranteed sub-60-second physical security response.
📡
FASTag Integration
Our entry gates are fully integrated with the NHAI FASTag RFID ecosystem. Vehicle registration, booking status, and slot number are verified and displayed automatically within two seconds of arrival — completely contactless, completely cashless, every single time.
💳
100% Cashless Facility
Autospot Parking is a zero-cash facility. Every transaction — from hourly bookings to annual subscription plans — is processed digitally through your FASTag-linked bank account. Cleaner, faster, completely fraud-proof, and hassle-free every single visit.
🛡️
Your Vehicle, Our Responsibility
We operate with 24/7 manned security, layered CCTV surveillance, individual slot-level alarms, FASTag-based identity verification at entry and exit, and strict no-loitering policies. The unauthorized removal of any registered vehicle from our premises is virtually impossible.
